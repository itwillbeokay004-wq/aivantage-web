# AiVantage Legal Review Checklist

This file is a practical handoff for legal review. It is not legal advice.

## Pages to Review

- `/privacidad` and `/en/privacy`
- `/terminos` and `/en/terms`
- Contact and demo forms that collect user-submitted information
- Email confirmation copy sent through Resend
- Analytics placeholders and future tracking configuration
- AI chat widget copy, especially if real AI mode is enabled later

## Privacy Policy Review Points

- Confirm what personal information AiVantage actually collects.
- Confirm how form submissions are stored, processed, and retained.
- Confirm whether analytics, cookies, PostHog, Google Analytics, or similar tools will be used.
- Confirm all third-party processors, including hosting, email delivery, CRM, analytics, and AI providers.
- Confirm data retention periods and deletion-request handling.
- Confirm contact email and business entity details.
- Confirm jurisdiction-specific privacy requirements for target markets.

## Terms of Service Review Points

- Confirm website-use restrictions and acceptable-use language.
- Confirm language for demo/contact requests and no-client-relationship disclaimers.
- Confirm no-guarantee language for AI agents, automations, integrations, and business outcomes.
- Confirm intellectual-property ownership and content-use terms.
- Confirm limitation-of-liability language.
- Confirm governing law, venue, and entity details.
- Confirm whether separate client contracts are required before paid work starts.

## Production Notes

- Keep visible template disclaimers on Privacy and Terms until legal review is complete.
- Do not claim compliance, certifications, partnerships, or guarantees unless verified.
- If real AI chat mode is enabled, review AI-specific disclosures, data handling, and escalation language.
- If analytics is enabled, update privacy copy to describe actual tracking behavior.
